Intro

Welcome to the portfolio website of Shazni Ahamed, a BSc Chemical and Process Engineering graduate. With a strong foundation in engineering and a passion for data analytics and business intelligence, I am dedicated to utilizing my skills and knowledge to drive innovation and problem-solving in challenging working environments. Throughout my academic journey at the University of Moratuwa, I have honed my technical skills in Python, JavaScript, MySQL, HTML, Tableau, Power BI, and MS Excel. These tools, combined with my analytical mindset, allow me to transform complex data into actionable insights, providing effective business solutions for success. I am particularly interested in the fields of data analytics, business analytics, food and environmental engineering, mathematics, and software engineering..

In addition to my academic achievements, I have actively participated in research and presented papers at international conferences. One of my notable accomplishments includes presenting a paper entitled "Modelling and Economic Analysis of Recovering Acetonitrile from Wastewater using Adsorption and Membrane Pervaporation" at the 9th International Symposium on Advances in Civil and Environmental Engineering Practices for Sustainable Development. I have also been recognized on the Dean's list during my sixth semester. As a freelancer, I have gained valuable experience in transforming data into actionable insights, contributing to the success of businesses. With a strong commitment to continuous learning and professional development, I am eager to take on new challenges and make a positive impact in the field of engineering and data analytics.
